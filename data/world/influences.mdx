Uh-oh, I've been worldbuilding under the influence.

Here are a few of them, in no particular order. I've definitely left out a lot. See my undergrad dissertation, _[On Yearning](/a/yearn)_, and my photobook, _[Lingermyth](/a/myth)_, for related ideas.

### Books

- Ursula K. Le Guin's [Earthsea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earthsea).
- Hope Mirrlees's [Lud-in-the-Mist](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lud-in-the-Mist).
- [On Fairy Stories](https://archive.org/details/on-fairy-stories_202110), Tolkien's lecture-turned-essay on the nature of fairytales.
- E. R. Eddison's [The Worm Ouroboros](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Worm_Ouroboros).

### Films

- The [French Dispatch](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_French_Dispatch) and others from Wes Anderson.
- [Princess Mononoke](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Princess_Mononoke), among other Studio Ghibli stuff.
- The 1977 animated [Hobbit](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hobbit_(1977_film)) movie.

### Art

- Picasso, especially his [little doodles](https://www.pablopicasso.org/drawings.jsp).
- The amazing art of [Troika](https://www.troikarpg.com/).
- Otherworldly landscapes by [Mœbius](https://surrealismtoday.com/jean-giraud-moebius/).
- [Tarot decks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rider%E2%80%93Waite_Tarot), and other funny old illustrations.

### Music

- [Bob Dylan](https://open.spotify.com/artist/74ASZWbe4lXaubB36ztrGX?si=4d76ced2a2ed4dd3), songs like _One More Cup of Coffee_, _Boots of Spanish Leather_, _All Along the Watchtower_, and _The Changing of the Guards_.
- [Andrés Segovia](https://open.spotify.com/artist/3Din7QXYnTjT52WF62KS97?si=9e5188d5b8e6466c), especially _Romance de los Pinos_ and _Recuerdos de la Alhambra_.
- More [here](https://open.spotify.com/playlist/3dPV2f6XlxbnYfCa1AXnHL?si=8f6cadb70ba94c05).
